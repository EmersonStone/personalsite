An Introduction

Hello, I'm a designer living in Boulder, CO. Currently, I spend my time working on branding and interactive projects with a few select companies. I love good typography and bad movies.

For the last eight years, I've been creating interactive products for startups and national brands. More recently, I've focused my efforts on digital product design for a few select clients here in Colorado. On the side, I am building an app for restaurant owners called Bistro and an event-based iPhone app that's still in the works. If you'd like to chat about your next design project, get in touch.
